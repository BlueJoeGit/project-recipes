Chef_John´s_Perfect_Prime_Rib

Description

Use my easy "mathematical method for cooking prime rib, and you´ll be rewarded with the best prime rib thats perfectly pink and delicious. For the math to work, you must leave the beef out at room temperature for at least 6 hours.

Tips

Formula: Cooking time (minutes) = rib´s weight x 5

After the cooking time is over, wait 2 hours before opening the oven

Ingredients

  • 1 bone-in prime rib roast
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 tablespoon freshly grounf black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon herbes of Provence
  • kosher salt to taste

Steps

  1. Place prime rib roast on a plate and bring to room temperature , 2 to 4 hours
  2. Preheat the oven at 500 260 degrees Celsius
  3. Combine butter, pepper, and herbes de Provence in a bowl; mix until well blended Spread butter mixture evenly over entire roast. Season roast generously with kosher salt.
  4. Roast the rib in the oven for (see formula above)
  5. After the cooking time, leave the roast int he oven for 2 hours
  6. Remove the roast form the oven, slice, and serve
